Lucy Griffiths

Styles: Ballet, Jazz & Troupes

Training
Lucy began her training at a local school in her hometown, she auditioned successfully to begin training at the Royal Ballet from age 11 to age 18. Lucy has high distinctions in Advanced 2 Ballet and Advanced 2 Jazz as well as various other qualificaitons including her Royal Ballet accredited teacher's degree

Professional experience
Lucy went stright from finishing her training with the Royal Ballet right into their company were she quickly rose through their ranks to become the principal ballerina. Lucy has also performed with Alvin Ailiey, The American Ballet Company and was one of only a few ballerinas invited to perform at the Bolshoi in a special performace of the nutcracker.
